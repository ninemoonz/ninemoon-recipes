Spicy Ramyun

Korean Istant Ramyun Noodle

Korean Soul Food

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Korean ramen
  • 1 egg
  • Water
  • Sliced cheese

Steps

  1. Boil 100ml of water
  2. Once the water boils, put the noodle and ramyun powder in the pot
  3. When the noodles are almost done, add one egg in the pot
  4. Boil it about 1 minute on the medium fire with lid on the pot
  5. Move the ramyun into a bowl, and top with a sliced cheese at the last
  6. Side with kimchi will perfect the meal.
