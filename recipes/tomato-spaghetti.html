Tomato Spaghetti

Classic Italian Tomato Pasta

Simple yet delicious gourmet everyone likes. Easy to cook, happy to eat

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce 2 can
  • Tomato paste 1 can
  • 2 Onions
  • Olive oil (A LOT)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pasta (Anyway you like)
  • Cheese Powder

Steps

  1. Chop two onions
  2. Pour two cup of olive oil on the pan
  3. Stir chopped onions on the pan for 15 minutes (Until onions get soggy)
  4. Add 2 cans of tomato sauce and 1 can of tomato paste on the pan
  5. Add some salt and pepper
  6. Boil the pasta noodles in the salt water
  7. When the pasta noodles are done, put it on the dish with tomato sauce
